Grit Chew

A high quality modern toboccoless dip company.

Original Grit Homepage
Redesign Grit Homepage

We had about 3 weeks to complete the whole project. We first reviewed old interviews, created a survey, a mood board, used an empathy map and storyboard to understand the users better. We then dove into the wireframing for a new redesign for the website which molded into our prototypes and then hi fidelity redesign. We did a redesign for both the mobile and desktop web versions. This project was quite dynamic and intrigueing, as none of us had used this form of tobacco chew before.

We had a 5 person team. Each with equal input and roles. We went through most of the deliverables together and presented as a team as well.

Without much personal understand of connection to the product, we had to look elsewhere during our research. We used old interviews, and connected with people via social media such as Reddit and linkedin, to get the info we needed.

Figma, Miro, Slack, Zoom, Grit Chew website, Google drive.

If we had had more time we would have expanded on our copy and content within the site, as well as re thought a bit more our style guide and color scheme.
